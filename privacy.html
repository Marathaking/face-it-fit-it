PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Obligations

Our Privacy Policy sets out the obligations and policies of Australian Fitness Management Pty Ltd, an Australian registered company and the Global Franchisor of Plus Fitness, as well as its International Master Franchisees, Franchisees, subsidiaries, affiliates, and associated companies (Plus Fitness) regardless of their location in the world. In particular, this policy details how we collect, use, disclose, store and enable access to your personal information, be that members, franchisees or employees, including both offline and online.

Information

Personal information includes any information or opinion about an individual who is reasonably identifiable regardless of whether the information is true or not. Plus Fitness only collects the personal information, which at times includes sensitive information (health information) that it deems necessary for it’s operations or activities. In general terms, the types of Personal Information collected by us may include, amongst other things your; name, address, date of birth, mobile and other telephone numbers, e-mail address, credit card or bank account details, occupation and employer, driver’s licence number, relevant national identity card details, emergency contact details, your image if required for membership identification purposes. For potential franchisees, additional information of a financial, professional and personal nature is required to be provided to Plus Fitness. For potential employees, any application and personal information provided to Plus Fitness will be used solely for the purpose of assessing the suitability for the position applied for. Personal information provided will only form part of an employee record if the application is successful.

Use of Personal Information

Personal information collected about individuals will be used and disclosed by us to provide the services required or otherwise to enable us to carry out our operations and activities. Plus Fitness will only disclose Personal Information to third a party business partner if they provide services to Plus Fitness that are consistent with the terms of this Privacy Policy. Where certain services are outsourced or contracted out our contractors may access an individuals’ Personal Information. Plus Fitness contractors are subject to strict confidentiality obligations. Plus Fitness ensures that it only engages with reputable business partners. Individuals consent to the use and disclosure of their Personal Information where it is incidental to a sale of any Plus Fitness business to a third party. They also consent to Personal Information being disclosed to the Plus Fitness Global Franchisor and its employees regardless of which Plus Fitness they join or attend. On request, Plus Fitness is obliged to disclose personal information if it is a legal requirement to do so in the region where the request is made.
